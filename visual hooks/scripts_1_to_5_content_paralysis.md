# SCRIPT #1 — "Tumhare paas product hai, store hai, customers bhi hai — but content ke naam pe ZERO"

**Pillar:** 1 — "The Business Reality No One Tells You"
**Platform:** IG Reel / YT Short
**Duration:** 50s
**Objective:** Trust — wake-up call for invisible businesses
**Target Segment:** Retail Chain Owners doing ₹1Cr+ but posting nothing online
**Angle:** Confrontation — calling out the gap between offline success and zero online presence
**Tone:** Direct, challenging, empathetic. Like a mentor slapping sense into them.

---

### HOOK (0-3 seconds):
**Visual:** Close-up of face, dead serious expression, dark moody background. Single light source from above creating dramatic shadows. Leaning slightly toward camera. One finger raised.
**Audio:** "₹1Cr ka business. Online presence? ZERO. Yeh tum ho."
**Text Overlay:** Bold white on black — "₹1Cr Revenue. ₹0 Online Presence."

---

### SETUP (3-12 seconds):
**Visual:** Pull back to medium shot. Still dark background. Arms crossed. Speaking directly to camera with intensity but not anger — more like concern.
**Dialogue:**
"Main 8 saal se business owners ke saath kaam kar raha hun. Aur ek pattern baar baar dikhta hai.

Revenue ₹30 lakh, ₹50 lakh, ₹1 crore monthly. Store pe customers aate hain. Products solid hain. Team hai. Sab hai.

Lekin online? Instagram dead. YouTube zero. Google pe competitor zyada dikhta hai. Koi content nahi. Koi presence nahi."

---

**Visual:** Now walking slowly. Camera follows. B-roll cuts interleaved: (a) a busy retail store with customers, (b) the SAME store's dead Instagram page with last post 4 months ago, (c) a competitor's active Instagram with reels and engagement. Quick cuts — the contrast hits visually.
**Dialogue:**
"Aur phir wohi business owner mujhse kehta hai — 'Bhai, marketing kaam nahi karta humare industry mein.'

Achha? Phir tumhara competitor kaise kar raha hai? Woh online kyun dikh raha hai? Uske customers Instagram se kyun aa rahe hain?

Suno dhyan se — tumhara business offline strong hai. Lekin offline ALONE ab kaam nahi karega. Tumhare customers PEHLE phone pe dekhte hain, PHIR store aate hain. Agar tum phone pe nahi dikhte — toh tum exist nahi karte unke liye.

₹1 crore ka business invisible hai. Yeh sochne wali baat hai."

**Text Overlay at key moments:**
- "Tumhara competitor kyun dikh raha hai?" (when mentioned)
- "Pehle PHONE pe dekhte hain. Phir STORE aate hain." (bold emphasis)
- "₹1Cr ka business. INVISIBLE." (climax of this section)

---

**Visual:** Stop walking. Face camera. Close-up. Quieter now — leaning in. The energy shifts from intensity to directness.
**Dialogue:**
"Main tumhe yeh nahi bol raha ki roz 10 reels daalo. Main bol raha hun — SHURU karo. Ek video. Ek post. Apni dukaan dikhao. Apne product dikhao. Apne customers ki story batao. Itna kaafi hai start ke liye.

Lekin KUCH toh karo. Kyunki jo nahi dikhta — woh bikta nahi."

### VISUAL HOOKS (5 Options):

**Hook 1 — THE NUMBER DROP**
Visual: Black screen. Bold text slams in: "₹1Cr Revenue." Beat. Then second line: "₹0 Online Presence." The contrast between the two numbers creates immediate tension.
Audio: Dramatic bass hit with each text slam. Then voice: "Yeh tum ho."

**Hook 2 — THE CONFRONTATION**
Visual: Close-up face, dark background, one overhead light. Serious expression. Points at camera.
Audio: "₹1Cr ka business. Online presence? ZERO. Yeh tum ho."
Text overlay: "Yeh TUM ho."
Why it works: Direct confrontation + specific revenue number = the TG immediately self-identifies. Pointing at camera makes it personal.

**Hook 3 — THE COMPARISON**
Visual: Split screen — LEFT: a busy retail store (customers, transactions, alive). RIGHT: the same store's dead Instagram page (no posts, no engagement). The contrast is stark.
Audio: "Store mein bheed hai. Instagram pe? Koi nahi jaanta tum exist karte ho."
Text overlay: "Offline: ₹1Cr. Online: ₹0."
Why it works: The visual contrast between thriving offline and dead online is undeniable. Every retail chain owner has experienced this exact gap.

**Hook 4 — THE REVEAL**
Visual: Phone screen recording — scrolling through a competitor's active Instagram (professional content, reels, engagement, comments). Then swipe to another profile — dead, no posts. Zoom into the dead profile's follower count and last post date.
Audio: "Yeh tumhara competitor hai. Aur yeh tum ho. Farak dikh raha hai?"
Text overlay: None needed — the screen recording IS the hook.
Why it works: Shows them their exact situation acted out on screen. The competitor comparison triggers their #1 fear — losing market share to someone more visible.

**Hook 5 — THE QUESTION**
Visual: Standing in a retail store (not a studio). Surrounded by products. Looking directly at camera.
Audio: "Tumhara store ₹1Cr ka revenue karta hai. Lekin agar main Google pe search karun — miloge? Instagram pe dhundhun — dikhoge? Nahi na?"
Text overlay: "Can customers find you online?"
Why it works: Asking the question in THEIR environment (a retail store) makes it impossible to dismiss. The question is rhetorical — they know the answer is no.

### RECOMMENDED: Hook 3 — The split screen between a thriving store and dead Instagram is the most visually powerful and instantly communicates the core message without needing any words.

**Angle:** Myth-busting each excuse (no time, don't know what to post, not photogenic, agency will handle, etc.)
**Tone:** Challenging but helpful. Each slide = one excuse destroyed.

---

**Headline:** "7 Excuses. 7 Lies."
**Sub-text:** "Yeh excuses tumhe content se rok rahe hain — aur tumhara market share kha rahe hain."

**Headline:** "Time nahi hai."

**Headline:** "Humare industry mein content kaam nahi karta."

# SCRIPT #5 — "Yeh hai asli process — pehle din se aaj tak"

**Pillar:** 5 — "Behind the Scenes at 2XG"
**Platform:** Instagram Stories
**Frames:** 7
**Objective:** Trust + Humanization + Relationship
**Target Segment:** All segments
**Angle:** Behind the scenes of 2XG's own content journey. Show the messy beginning vs. now.
**Tone:** Raw, authentic, vulnerable

---

### FRAME 1 (15 sec):
**Type:** Text on dark photo + Poll sticker
**Content:**
Photo: Blurry, low-quality BTS photo from Day 1 of content creation (or recreated mock — phone on a stack of books as tripod, messy desk, bad lighting).
Text overlay: "Yeh tha humara Day 1 setup. Seriously."
**Sticker/Interactive:** Poll — "Tumhara setup abhi aisa hai? YES / WORSE"

---

### FRAME 2 (15 sec):
**Type:** Video selfie — raw, talking to camera
**Content:**
Speaking directly to camera, handheld. Slightly shaky. Real.
Dialogue: "Main aaj kuch honest share karna chahta hun. Jab humne 2XG ka content shuru kiya — humein kuch nahi aata tha. KUCH nahi. Pehla video 17 baar record kiya. 17 baar. Phir bhi cringe tha."
**Sticker/Interactive:** None — let the story breathe.

---

### FRAME 3 (15 sec):
**Type:** Screenshot/photo dump — early content vs. now
**Content:**
Side by side:
LEFT: Screenshot of first Instagram post (mock: bad graphic, 3 likes, generic caption).
RIGHT: Recent post (mock: professional reel, high engagement).
Text overlay: "Same account. Same person. Sirf consistency ka farak."
**Sticker/Interactive:** Slider — "Kitna farak hai?" 0 to Max

---

### FRAME 4 (15 sec):
**Type:** Video selfie — emotional, closer to camera
**Content:**
Dialogue: "First month mein maine 12 videos daale. Views? 30-50 per reel. Koi comment nahi. Koi DM nahi. Main sochta tha — yeh kaam nahi kar raha.

Lekin maine band nahi kiya."
**Sticker/Interactive:** None — emotional beat. Let it sit.

---

### FRAME 5 (15 sec):
**Type:** Screen recording of current analytics
**Content:**
Show: Instagram Insights dashboard (mock/placeholder). Scrolling through — showing follower growth graph, reach increasing month over month, DMs coming in.
Text overlay: "6 months later. Same effort. Different numbers."
**Sticker/Interactive:** None — the data speaks.

---

### FRAME 6 (15 sec):
**Type:** Video selfie — sitting, relaxed, grounded
**Content:**
Dialogue: "Aaj 300K+ followers hain. Clients inbound aate hain. Content se business banta hai — yeh main apne experience se bol raha hun.

Lekin yeh sab tabhi hua jab maine PEHLA bakwas video daala. Agar main woh nahi daalta — yeh sab nahi hota."
**Sticker/Interactive:** None — landing the message.

---

### FRAME 7 (15 sec):
**Type:** Text on solid color background + Question sticker
**Content:**
Background: Solid dark background or branded 2XG dark template.
Text: "Tumhara pehla video kab daaloge?

Nahi — 'jaldi' nahi. Date batao."
**Sticker/Interactive:** Question sticker — "EXACT date batao — pehla video kab? Main dekhta hun."

---

### STORY ARC:
VULNERABILITY (messy start) → STRUGGLE (no results early) → PROOF (current success) → CHALLENGE (your turn — when?). The arc is designed to make the viewer think: "If he started this messy and got here, I can too." The final frame forces commitment by asking for a specific date.

### ENGAGEMENT HOOK:
Frame 1's poll gets instant taps. Frame 7's question sticker drives direct DM replies — which become warm leads. The slider in Frame 3 is a low-effort interaction that boosts story completion rate.

# PRODUCTION NOTES — SCRIPTS 1-5

**Batch Theme:** Content Paralysis / Getting Started

**Cross-Script Strategy:**
- Script 1 creates AWARENESS of the gap (you're invisible online)
- Script 2 destroys EXCUSES systematically (save-worthy carousel)
- Script 3 creates EMOTIONAL URGENCY through competitor jealousy
- Script 4 removes PERFECTIONISM as a barrier
- Script 5 builds TRUST by showing your own messy journey

**Recommended Posting Order:** 5 → 1 → 4 → 2 → 3
(Start with vulnerability/BTS story to warm audience, then confrontation, then encouragement, then educational carousel, then the sharp closer.)

**Pillar Distribution:**
- Pillar 1: 3 scripts (60%) — justified for "getting started" batch
- Pillar 4: 1 script (20%) — polarization piece
- Pillar 5: 1 script (20%) — BTS humanization

**DM Triggers:** START, NO EXCUSE, SHURU, DONE
