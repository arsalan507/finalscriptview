## SERIES DESIGN CANVAS

### SERIES THESIS

> *\"I will take Bangalore parents through the REAL journey of a father deciding to buy an e-cycle for his son - from first denial to final purchase - so they can see themselves in every episode and feel compelled to visit BCH, through a 15-episode character-driven comedy-drama series.\"*

  --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Factor**              **Standalone Scripts**                 **This Series**
  ----------------------- -------------------------------------- -----------------------------------------------------------------
  Trust Building          1 exposure = \"interesting\"           15 exposures = \"I trust BCH implicitly\"

  Algorithm Reward        Random topic = confused distribution   Topic consistency = confident promotion

  Identity Bonding        None                                   After Ep 3+, viewers self-identify as \"Appa, Please! watcher\"

  CAC Impact              Same cost per customer                 60% reduction over 12 months (compounding)

  Conversion Rate         2-5% cold                              15-25% from series (3-5 eps watched), 30-50% (10+ eps)

  \"Part 2?\" Effect      None                                   FOMO + completion compulsion + community participation
  --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### VIRAL SCIENCE APPLIED (From Mini-Series Research)

> 1\. **Zeigarnik Effect:** Open loops between episodes drive \"must watch next\" behavior
>
> 2\. **Identity Bonding:** After 3+ episodes, viewers defend the series, recommend to similar parents
>
> 3\. **Habit Formation:** Consistent M-W-F posting = brain anticipates next episode
>
> 4\. **Dopamine + Familiarity Balance:** Same format (comfort) + new content each ep (novelty) + cliffhangers (anticipation)
>
> 5\. **Algorithm Memory:** Series performs well = algorithm confidently promotes ALL future BCH content

**\[0:32-0:40\] THE COMEDY BEAT - Appa\'s Logic**

**\[0:48-0:53\] SERIES CLOSER**
